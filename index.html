Short CV

I am currently a Staff Research Scientist and TLM at Google Translate in Mountain View, CA. My current research interests are in machine translation, focusing on human and automatic evaluation, decoding strategies, model training, and data processing. Prior to joining Google, I worked as a Research Staff Member at IBM in Yorktown Heights, NY. I received a PhD in Computer Science in 2015 from the RWTH Aachen University under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Hermann Ney.
